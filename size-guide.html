How to Measure

01 / Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and comfortably loose.

02 / Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, inline with your navel, keeping the tape slightly relaxed.

03 / Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 7-8 inches below your waistline.
