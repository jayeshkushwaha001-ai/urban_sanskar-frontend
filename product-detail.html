Wash and Care

Treat your garment with care to enjoy it for years.

  • Cold hand wash separately.
  • Mild detergent only.
  • Dry in shade.
  • Warm iron.
  • Do not bleach or tumble dry.
Size & Customisation

Our ready-to-ship pieces are available in S, M, and L. Need a different size? We offer complimentary size customisation at no additional cost to ensure a better fit.

Please note that design or pattern customisations may vary depending on the garment. If you'd like to discuss alterations, we'd be happy to help before you place your order.

Have questions about alterations? Contact us on WhatsApp (+91 9746539916)

Returns & Exchanges Strict Policy

We offer size exchange, style exchange, or store credit only.

Strictly no monetary refunds are issued.

  • Returns or exchange requests must be initiated within 3 days of delivery.
  • Any purchase can be exchanged for a different size. If your required size is unavailable, we will issue a store credit valid for six months from the date of issue.
  • Please check our size chart carefully before purchasing. For any questions regarding size, fabric, or fit, contact us immediately.
Damaged or Defective Items

All orders go through a strict, multi-level quality check before dispatch.

  • Returns for damaged products must be strictly pre-approved via email. Any items sent back without prior email approval will not be accepted at our studio.
  • We accept the physical transit return within 10 days once the approval is given.
Important Notes
  • Shipping Costs: The cost of return shipping logistics for size exchanges or damaged product returns is to be borne entirely by the customer.
  • Final Sale: All discounted pieces, clearance items, and custom orders are final sale and cannot be exchanged or returned unless explicitly proven defective.
